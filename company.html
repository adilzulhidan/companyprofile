About Us

ZulhidanCorp is a leading company focused on developing and implementing Internet of Things (IoT) solutions for various industrial sectors. Founded in 2020, we are committed to delivering advanced technologies that connect devices, systems, and people to create a more efficient, intelligent, and sustainable ecosystem.

Vision: To be a leader in IoT innovation that connects the world in a smarter, more efficient, and more sustainable way.

Mission: Technology Innovation, Improving Quality of Life, Sustainability, Education and Awareness, Strategic Partnerships.
